Verbs:

It is important to learn the different forms of a verb to speak Spanish correctly. The following table has 4 forms of the root word.
1) Infinitive (to + verb)
2) First person (Yo ...)
3) Second person (Tu ....)
4)Third person (Ella, El, Usted)
